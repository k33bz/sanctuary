# Feral Eggs — breeding the apocalypse, one hen at a time

*A Zugzuggin' original.*

Rabid hens lay **Feral Eggs**: still vanilla eggs, named plainly "Feral Egg" — the **name
color is the tier** (gold Savage, red Ferocious, dark red Nightmare) and the **gold stars in
the tooltip are the quality**. Colors can't be forged on an anvil; fakes stay fake. Dropped
feral eggs smolder with a faint red mote.

## The gamble

Vanilla's 1-in-8 egg→chick odds are untouched. When a chick does hatch from a feral egg, the
star table decides what it is (normal chick / bloodline tier −1 / same / +1):

| Stars | Normal | Down | Same | Up |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0★ (wild hen) | 90% | — | 9% | 1% |
| 1★ | 75% | 5% | 19% | 1% |
| 2★ | 66% | 8% | 24% | 2% |
| 3★ | 50% | 10% | 35% | 5% |
| 4★ | 33% | 10% | 47% | 10% |
| 5★ (cap) | 25% | 10% | 45% | 20% |

Clamped Savage..Nightmare — a bloodline never breeds back to calm.

## The bloodline

- A destined chick hatches **calm** and looks completely normal. The generation hides in its
  blood — **never shown on the bird** — and only reveals itself on the eggs it lays later.
- It **turns** the first time it loads as an adult (chunk reload / restart), anywhere.
- Each destined generation lays eggs **one star deeper**, cap 5★. Five lucky generations
  stand between a wild Savage hen and true 5★ Nightmare stock.
- Stat boards track `sanct_gen_best` — the server's breeding crown.

## Frontier ranching (by design)

A pacified bloodline hen inside a sanctuary lays **plain** eggs. Starred eggs only come from
an actively feral hen — which only happens **outside** sanctuary ground. Store your stock
safely at home; to harvest, run a coop in the wilds and defend it. Sanctuaries are literally
that: safe. Risk is the price of Nightmare poultry.

Tips: a Golden Dandelion pauses a chick's aging — a living bloodline vault. Eggs a dropper
feeds a feral hen also turn: not a bug, a farm. Config: `feralEggsEnabled` under `mobscaling`.
