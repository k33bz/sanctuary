Sanctuary bundles datapacks and crafting recipes inspired by / derived from:

Vanilla Tweaks: https://vanillatweaks.net/

- "Sanctuary Crafting Tweaks" (sanctuary:crafting_tweaks) is Sanctuary's own
  datapack of crafting recipes popularised by the Vanilla Tweaks Crafting
  Tweaks. Crafting recipes are not copyrightable; this pack is maintained as
  part of Sanctuary, with Vanilla Tweaks credited here for the concepts.
- The remaining bundled datapacks (sanctuary:vt/<name>) are redistributed from
  Vanilla Tweaks within this substantially larger free project per the Vanilla
  Tweaks terms of use (https://vanillatweaks.net/terms/), with attribution here
  and on the project's download pages.

All bundled packs are disabled by default and opt-in per world via
/datapack enable.
