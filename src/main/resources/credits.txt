Sanctuary bundles a selection of datapacks and crafting tweaks from:

Vanilla Tweaks: https://vanillatweaks.net/

All bundled packs are disabled by default and opt-in per world via
/datapack enable "sanctuary:vt/<name>". They are redistributed within this
substantially larger free project per the Vanilla Tweaks terms of use
(https://vanillatweaks.net/terms/), with attribution here and on the
project's download pages.
